Solutions for Logistics and Transportation Providers

The global economy has evolved from just logistics to x-shoring, omni-channel business models, global transportation requirements and energy efficient supply chains. Today, transportation and logistics services providers face a new set of challenges:

  • Focus on the Internet of Moving Things (IoMT) with every freight- and passenger-carrying vehicle envisaged to be in real time communication with distributed cloud-enabled network control centers
  • Widespread adoption of APIs and web service integration for flexible, dynamic and collaborative business models
  • Event handling and analytics for self-corrective and intelligent disruption of management processes

It is imperative for logistics and transportation service providers to have a transparent view of their supply chains, systems and process to cater to omni-channel business models and drive business and operational efficiency to stay competitive. In their quest to reduce infrastructure costs, mainframe-based logistics businesses are seeking to move certain functions to new generation multi-tiered API-based architecture with a responsive Web UI. Legacy digitization initiatives are helping deliver personalized information to new gen employee and customer devices, enhancing their overall interaction experience.

Syntel helps logistics and transportation companies overcome these challenges by providing better collaboration, efficiency and customer satisfaction. From distribution channels to customer management and sales, Syntel drives informed decision-making to improve process efficiency and customer-centricity.
