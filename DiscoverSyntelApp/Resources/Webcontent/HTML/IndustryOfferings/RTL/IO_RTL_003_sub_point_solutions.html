Store Service Workbench (SSW)

There are any number of tasks that may keep store associates from spending enough time assisting customers face-to-face. This may be attributable to poor automation levels at stores, improper task management and multiple information systems.

Syntel’s Store Service Workbench (SSW) is a solution that enables both headquarters and store associates to channelize, track and manage store operations. It enables retailers to minimize labor costs and simplify task complexity, as well as retain informed customers, shorten product lifecycles and overcome other operational challenges.

Key Features

  • Metrics dashboards
  • Flexible reporting
  • Project management capabilities to streamline operations
  • Effective store process compliance
  • Rule-based alerts
  • Seamless convergence across devices
  • NRF Arts-based data model for easy adoption
  • Reuses MS Office applications
  • Requires no changes with existing store systems investments

Shopper Assist

Consumers today rely heavily on smart phones to access information, socialize and make shopping decisions. Syntel’s Shopper Assist is a consumer mobile application that enables retailers to enhance customer engagement and loyalty. It provides consumers the ability to maintain shopping lists and seamless access to product inventory. The application also has many built-in features that enable retailers to offer personalized consumer services.

Key Features

  • Seamless integration with e-Commerce and store channels
  • Aisle-based promotions
  • Customized product suggestions
  • In-store item locator
  • Scan to know feature
  • Route shopping list while in store
  • Social media integration
  • Mobile checkout
  • Digital wallet

BPTR – Business Process Test Repository

Syntel’s BPTR is a huge repository of retail-based business processes connected to an automation framework that accelerates the testing cycle and reduces SME dependence by utilizing more than 1,000 test scenarios.

BPTR automatically generates test cases and artifacts based on standard business processes, but also has the flexibility to be customized to meet each of our clients’ unique needs.

Key Features

  • 30-50% reduction in IT requirement gathering, design and testing costs
  • Faster time to market
  • Pre-populated testing templates for requirement gathering, test planning and testing strategy
  • Process traceability

Associate Tracker

An effective store manager needs to be able to put the right associates in the right place in their store. Not only does it enhance customer service with a better in-store experience for the consumer, but it also motivates associates and improves job satisfaction with assignments more suited to their particular skillsets.

Syntel’s Associate Tracker precisely tracks the locations of associates in the store, enabling more personalized customer service and dynamic workforce management.

Key Features

  • Understand employee workflows
  • Effective workforce management
  • Personalized customer service
  • Provides insights on customer patterns

Customer Personalization as a Service (CPaaS)

Retailers can leverage Big Data solutions to gain valuable insights into consumer preference, and offer personalized services to ensure consumer loyalty.

Syntel‘s CPaaS utilizes open source platforms harnessing petabytes of consumer data from multiple channels to deliver actionable insights for use in marketing campaigns and promotions that resonate with consumers’ unique preferences.

The platform enables retailers to build individual customer profiles and offer a tailor-made shopping experience. Furthermore, because CPaaS is built on an open source platform, it also delivers lower TCO and the flexibility to support a variety of different data sources.
