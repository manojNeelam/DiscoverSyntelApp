Internet Of Things

Today connected devices outnumber humans on the planet. Research suggests that by 2020, there will be over six connected devices per human. This anticipated growth underlines the importance of Internet enabled devices as a key driver for generating real-time actionable insights. Organizations need to focus on building Internet of Things (IoT) ready platforms.

Our Services

Syntel’s IoT Event Fabric provides a highly modular and scalable solution to build 'Internet of Things' applications. It is a smarter way of connecting devices, to enable smarter information sharing.

Syntel’s IoT Event Fabric offers scalability, enhanced productivity and reduced complexity. It leverages the Reactor Design Pattern for complete asynchronous processing. The solution offers plug-and-play interface adapters and has a cloud ready architecture, with external hosting features.

Salient Features

  • Supports thousands of connections from a plethora of smart devices
  • Better flexibility for multiple open or proprietary protocols and ‘signal’ data formats from various embedded devices
  • Ability to horizontally scale out the event processing as the number of devices on the internet increase
  • Ability to execute complex business rules on incoming events in real time
  • Ability to easily integrate with other business processes and applications
