Customer Insight

The second layer is customer insight, which enhances the customer experience by providing relevant, real-time and actionable information.

Customer analytics enables organizations to get a comprehensive and consistent view of the customer across internal and external information sources and drive insights into behavior, sentiment and preferences to drive targeted campaigns for retention, cross-sell, up-sell and enhancing lifetime customer value.

Streaming analytics focus on time, context and location specific decision support to drive real time customer interventions.
